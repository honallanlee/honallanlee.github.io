Hello! I am Allan Lee. I am now re-running for CGCU Treasurer.

Hi! My name is Allan, I'm a third year biomedical engineering student and yes I would like to run for CGCU treasurer again. There hasn’t been much going on this year due to COVID restrictions which is why I am re-running for next year inwith the hopes of providing the best CGCU experiences to everyone who missed out this year. My experience of being a treasurer dates back when I was in secondary school societies, secondary school's student association and more recently Imperial’s bioengineering society and this year’s CGCU.
I hope that I can carry build on the foundations laid by past treasurers and maintain the efficiency of this year’s management group, to be an integral part in one of the constituent committee, giving you the best that you can enjoy in a bigger CGCU Welcome dinner, social events (I am going to actively ask for more social events to be held so that people from different engineering disciplines can know each other, and to make it up for this year!!) and of course !! in hopes of bringing more welfare events too.
I am familiar with the processes such as asking for sales invoice, claims, purchase orders, drafting annual and event budgets. What I learnt from taking up this post, it is that the CGCU treasurer is an important bridge between CSP treasurers and the union, in speeding up financial authorisation procedures or checking paperworks before it was is processed by the union, and I would be more than willingly to be that bridge AGAIN.

My goals:
- Maintaining current efficiency in financial approvals for CSPs & CGCU
- Carefully work out the best I could do for event and annual budget
- Proactively reach out to CSP treasurer
- Return to normal spending on events
